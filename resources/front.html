About Glossa

  • Developed at the Text Laboratory, Dept. of Linguistics and Scandinavian Studies, University of Oslo with support from the Norwegian contribution to the CLARIN infrastructure, CLARINO.
  • Freely available for download from GitHub. Easy to install on your own server.
  • Search engine agnostic. Comes with support for the IMS Corpus Workbench and CLARIN federated content search out of the box.
